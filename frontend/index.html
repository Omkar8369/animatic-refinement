Export

Downloads metadata.json to your browser's Downloads folder. This is the canonical hand-off into Node 2 of the pipeline. The MP4 files themselves are not uploaded by this form - copy them into the pipeline input folder separately. Filenames in metadata.json must match the MP4 filenames you place there.
