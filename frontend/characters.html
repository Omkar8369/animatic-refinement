Step 1 - Pre-register every character in this project

Upload one model-sheet PNG per character. The sheet must be an 8-angle horizontal strip (transparent or solid background, full color). Add the character's display name (e.g. Bhim, Chutki). These names will populate the identity dropdown on the shot form.

DWPose is the default and right for human characters (Bhim, Chutki, Raju). Switch to LineArt/Scribble fallback for quadrupeds or other non-human characters whose skeletons DWPose cannot reliably extract (e.g. Jaggu the monkey).
